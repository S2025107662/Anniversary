To my boyfriend, who is also my best friend,

Happy anniversary, baby.

I still cannot believe that we made it to another chapter together, and honestly, I am so grateful that we did. We have been through so many fights, arguments, and misunderstandings throughout the year, but somehow, through all of it, we still found our way back to each other. That is something I will always be thankful for. I am grateful to have been with you for 1,093 days, and officially for 365 days. Every single one of those days, in one way or another, has become part of my heart.

I wanted to write this letter because there are things I feel for you that are too important to be said only in passing. Some feelings deserve to be written down, kept, and remembered. And if there is one thing I never want you to forget, it is how much you mean to me.

Thank you for your patience with me, especially during the days when I am hard to understand. Thank you for staying gentle with me when my moods suddenly change, when I isolate myself, when I do not feel like talking, and even when we argue. Thank you for still choosing to understand me even when I am not easy to handle. Thank you for taking care of me and always making sure I am taking care of myself too, even when I act annoyed about it, especially when you keep asking if I have already eaten. It may be annoying in the moment, but deep down, it makes me feel loved because it reminds me that you care about me in the little ways that matter most.

Thank you also for the simple things you do for me, the quiet things that may seem small to other people but mean so much to me. The way you carry my bag or whatever I am bringing, the way you make things easier for me without even being asked, and the way you treat me so gently and so carefully like I am your princess. Those little actions stay with me. They make me feel safe, loved, and taken care of. You have become my safe place, my comfort, and my rest.

I still remember how shy you were at first. It is funny to think about now because you would not even talk to me before. You were so quiet, and everything between us felt so soft and awkward in the sweetest way. Looking back at that version of us makes me smile, because who would have thought that the shy boy who could barely talk to me would become one of the most important people in my life?

Being with you has changed me in so many ways. You made me softer. You made me feel more understood. You showed me that love does not always have to be loud, painful, or complicated to be real. With you, I learned that love can also be peaceful. It can feel gentle, safe, and steady. Being with you also made me feel less alone. Even on my hardest days, there is comfort in knowing that I have you. That alone means more than I can ever fully explain.

I admire you for your effort, your heart, and your gentleness. I admire the way you keep showing up for me, even in the simplest ways, like checking on me, carrying things for me, making sure I am okay, and trying your best to understand me when things get difficult. I admire your heart because it is kind and sincere. And I admire your gentleness because you never make me feel like I am too much. Instead, you make me feel accepted, cared for, and loved.

As we celebrate this anniversary, I just want you to know that I still want more memories with you. I still want to keep choosing you, not just today, but in all the days that come after this. I hope that we continue to grow together, learn together, and become even stronger together. And just as you have been there for me, I also want to be there for you. I want to be someone who gives you comfort, love, and peace the same way you have given those things to me.

Thank you for being you. Thank you for loving me in all the ways that you do. Thank you for being my boyfriend, my best friend, my safe place, and one of the best parts of my life.

Happy anniversary, baby. I love you so much.

Love,
Nadine
